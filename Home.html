About Us

Relaxed? Always at Concorde.
Welcome to Singapore’s premier business hotel.
Located on the world renowned Orchard Road, an 800,000 square metre playground of retail, dining and entertainment surrounds you. From famous brands to gourmet cuisine, immerse yourself in the fast-paced city lifestyle with the satisfaction of a modern hotel to unwind in.

On our doorstep:
  • Chinatown
  • Suntec City
  • Marina South
  • The Esplanade
  • Clarke Quay
  • Boat Quay

Proudly displaying 407 exceptional rooms and suites, the Blue pearl Singapore is the height of luxury. Each room comes equipped with a purpose-built work area for our business-oriented traveller, yet holds enough personality and charm to satisfy guests of any background. Complementary Wi-Fi is available throughout our expansive property, easily accessible from your room, in the lobby, or by the pool.
For our esteemed guest not quite ready to trawl Orchard Road for excellent food, a visit to our Spices Cafe presents a succulent choice of local and Western cuisine. An extensive menu awaits, offering diners an incredible array of meat, seafood, vegetables and desserts. Our hotel is also home to a pool bar, cocktail lounge, and Japanese themed restaurant. For those searching for the true hotel experience, you’ve found it.
Always spoilt for choice. Always at Concorde.
Blue pearl Singapore is situated only 21km and 25 minutes away from Changi International Airport by car or taxi.
