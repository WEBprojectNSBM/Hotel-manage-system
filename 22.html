FACILITIES










The extensive selection of facilities at our exclusive beach resort in Singapore includes exciting indoor and outdoor resort activities in addition to recreational facilities such as our all equipped gym and our spacious swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy revitalizing wellness treatments in our tranquil spa after splurging to their heart’s content at our exclusive shopping arcade. Discover all this and more at Blue Pearl, catering to your most dearest desires. Your comfort and convenience is utmost in our mind, always.


RESORT ACTIVITIES

Reside at our Chilake resort and be escorted to a host of exciting indoor and outdoor activities that can be enjoyed with those who matter to you the most. Begin with a quiet game of chess in the morning before heading out to the waters of the Indian Ocean for some deep sea fishing in the afternoon. One may also choose the comfort of one’s room to catch the latest blockbuster movies while being treated to the warm hospitality dished out in abundance.
