LOCATION










The extensive selection of facilities at our exclusive beach resort in Singapore includes exciting indoor and outdoor resort activities in addition to recreational facilities such as our all equipped gym and our spacious swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy revitalizing wellness treatments in our tranquil spa after splurging to their heart’s content at our exclusive shopping arcade. Discover all this and more at Hotel Blue Pearl, catering to your most dearest desires. Your comfort and convenience is utmost in our mind, always.


ATTRACTIONS









Flanked by the Indian Ocean and Chilaw lagoon, Hotel Blue Pearl provides the perfect backdrop for those searching for a refreshing and rejuvenating retreat. This Chilake beach hotel paves the way for guests to enjoy a truly memorable holiday as it is found within close proximity to a number of exciting destinations that are testament to the island’s rich heritage.

TRANSPORTATION









Discover luxury in the midst of the quaint little town of Karukupone, located around 66km north of the Singapore International Airport, which is an hour and thirty minutes’ drive up the pristine coastline. The hotel is also 7km (a 20-minute drive) north of the vibrant town of Chilake, which is renowned for its rich spiritual, cultural, and scenic attractions. Guests who fly in have a variety of Chilake travel options to consider. You can hire an airport taxi from SIA, or for those with large families and ample baggage, airport vans are also available at reasonable fares. Alternatively, you can hop aboard a bus or a train, the public bus station being located around 1km from the airport. These buses are quite comfortable, some being intercity A/C buses providing a safe and comfortable option that will bring you to your respective terminals at Chilaw. From there, you have the option of hailing a trishaw that will bring you straight to Blue Pearl.
